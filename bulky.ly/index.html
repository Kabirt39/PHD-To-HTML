Can I try Bulkly before I pay for it?

Of course! All Bulkly accounts come with a free 7-day trial. If for some odd reason Bulkly isn’t for you, simply cancel before the trial is complete.

Is there a discount for paying annually?

We’re flexible. You can pay monthly or yearly. Pay for a full year and get 25% off.

What forms of payment are accepted?

Currently we accept Visa, Mastercard, and American Express.

Is Bulkly a secure website?

Yup! Bulkly uses SSL encryption across the whole site and is Level 1 PCI compliant.

Do I need a Buffer account to use Bulkly?

Yes. Bulkly does not publish social media updates. Instead, Bulkly automatically drips status updates to your Buffer account where Buffer will post it on your behalf.

Can I review content before it is published on my social media accounts?

Absolutely. All content can be reviewed in your Buffer queue before Buffer posts it at your next scheduled time.

Why would someone want to automate social media?

It might sound counterintuitive, but by automating the re-use of status updates not only can you expose new followers to your updates, but you save time manually doing the same tasks over and over. Ultimately you can free up more time to spend engaging with your audience.
