10-50lb Resistance Bands

The Straight Line System’s specialized resistance band is double dipped in latex for extended use and double anchored to ensure the handles are securely fastened to the band. The handles double as stir-ups for your feet during certain exercises and have a synching buckle on each handle for arch anchoring. The Straight Line resistance band is longer than the usual resistance band to give you multiple tension options, which increases the intensity of the workout.

30-80lb Resistance Bands

The Straight Line resistance band also features a comfort sleeve, which slides back and forth freely for comfort during certain exercises and determining foot positions. The Straight Line system is neatly packaged in a stylish durable mesh tote bag for easy travel.

Is the Straight Line right for me?

The Straight Line has been designed for multiple fitness levels.

Because of the line's length your able to go from light to medium or heavy with just chocking up on the line or wrapping the line around your hand for the desired resistance. This is for anybody who is looking to get in to better health and shape.

What resistance band should I order?

If you are just starting your fitness journey, I recommend starting off with 10-50 lbs band.

Even if your familiar with gym workouts and are pretty strong, I still suggest you start with the 10-50 to get used to the moves with the full range of motion.

When it come to the 30-80 lbs band, this geared to the person that has mastered their fitness levels and wants to really,r eally push their limits.
